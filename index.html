Unraveling the Mystery.

So one time, during a particularly boring class in school, my friend and I decided to pass time by assigning colors to people based on their personality and the aura/vibe they seemed to give off. So apparently, being disgustingly cheerful, very funny, over-dramatic, creative and intelligent results in a 'sparkly/glittery bright orange.'
It all boils down to the fact that I’m a very bold, confident and extroverted person. I’m no stranger to judgmental glances and strange looks being thrown my way but I developed my thick skin years ago. Come what may, nothing will stop me from doing what I want to and standing up for what I believe in. I can summarize myself by saying that I’m generally full of life. I love meeting new people and interacting with people outside of my comfort zone. I’m also a very exciting person which results in crazy things happening every other day. My main goal in life is to convert as many ordinary moments into extraordinary events as possible.
Glittery orange it is then – after all, I do love standing out and shining bright.
